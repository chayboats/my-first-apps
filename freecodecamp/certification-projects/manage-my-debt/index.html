I created this site out of a recent obsession I had--personal finance!

I grew up not knowing anything about effectively managing money. After some time and research, I improved and found the enlightenment gratifying and freeing. One of the most benefical pieces of knowledge I gained was the avalanche debt repayment method.

The avalanche method aims to get rid of high-interest debts first. To do this, you will make minimum payments on all outstanding accounts equivalent to their interest rates. (This way, your interest will not build up over time.) All of the extra money you have saved for debt repayment will go towards paying off the debt with the highest interest. Once you've erased that debt, you move onto the next, and so on until you've completely erased all of your debt!
